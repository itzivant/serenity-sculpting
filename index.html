Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first session with Crystal.

How do I book an appointment?

Click Book Now on this page to schedule directly through GlossGenius. You can also DM Crystal on Instagram @serenitysculpting_ or text (559) 481-9143. Your appointment is only confirmed once your deposit is received.

How do I choose the right treatment?

DM or text Crystal with a brief description of your goals and concerns — she'll recommend the service that fits you best. Not sure? Book a consultation and she'll walk you through everything.

Can I eat before my session?

Yes — but keep it light. Eat something that sits easy on your stomach and avoid overeating or arriving feeling bloated, as this can affect your treatment results.

What should I wear?

Comfortable, loose clothing is best. Crystal will provide everything needed for your specific treatment during the session.

How many sessions will I need?

Most clients see visible results after just one session. For lasting transformation, a series of 4–8 sessions is typically recommended depending on your goals and the area being treated.

Is it painful?

No — all treatments are 100% non-invasive and comfortable. Most clients find the sessions deeply relaxing. There is no surgery and no recovery downtime.

How soon after giving birth can I come in?

At least 6 weeks after a natural birth, or 6 months after a C-section. Always consult your doctor before beginning treatments postpartum.

Can I receive treatment while breastfeeding?

Yes, but please pump and dump after your session. The treatments release stored toxins, which can enter your milk supply — so it's important to take this precaution to protect your baby.

What is your cancellation policy?

You may cancel or reschedule with 24+ hours notice at no charge. Less than 24 hours: 50% of the service price is charged. No-shows are charged 100% of the booked service. You may reschedule up to 2 times — after that, a new deposit is required.

What if I arrive late?

Please arrive 5–10 minutes early and text Crystal when you arrive — do not ring the doorbell. If you're more than 10 minutes late, your session will be cancelled and the deposit is forfeited.
